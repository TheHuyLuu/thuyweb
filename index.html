Info

Thuy is a creative working across illustration, visual styling, and graphic design. Moving between these disciplines allows her to approach each project with flexibility and a broad visual perspective.


After graduating in 2D Graphic Design in 2022, she began her career at Caay Creative Agency as an intern graphic designer. That same year, she joined Studio DUY and grew from assistant to art admin. Since late 2024, she has been collaborating with Đình Collective as a creative partner.


Along her journey, she works alongside Tom Huy — a graphic designer who is always learning and exploring. Outside of work, they have traveled across Vietnam and created @Theroadcat99 (MèoXaLộ™), a space where they document their shared experiences through illustration and graphic design.
